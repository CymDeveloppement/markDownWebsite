# Our History

Discover the history of our company and the milestones that marked our development.

## The beginnings (2012-2015)

Our adventure began in 2012 when Sophie Martin, freshly graduated, decided to create her own web agency. Starting from scratch with a clear vision: to offer quality digital solutions accessible to all businesses.

**2012** : Creation of the company with a team of 3 people  
**2013** : First satisfied clients and first fundraising  
**2014** : Opening of the first office in Paris  
**2015** : Growth to 10 employees and 50 clients

## The expansion (2016-2019)

The following years were marked by strong and structured growth. The company develops its offering and extends its scope of intervention.

**2016** : Launch of our digital consulting service  
**2017** : Opening of the Lyon office  
**2018** : Growth to 20 employees and 100 clients  
**2019** : National recognition with the "Best Web Agency" award

## Innovation (2020-2024)

The challenges of accelerated digital transformation pushed us to innovate more and strengthen our technical expertise.

**2020** : Adaptation and digitization of our internal processes  
**2021** : Launch of our premium e-commerce offering  
**2022** : Opening of the Bordeaux office  
**2023** : 150+ clients and 25 employees  
**2024** : Expansion of our services with a new cloud offering
